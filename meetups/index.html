meetup FAQs

Where can I find info on the next meet-up?

We post the meet-up details to our Twitter and our Discord. Find us there!


Where are the meet-ups held?

Meet-ups are currently being held at The Edge which is attached the State Library. The Edge is close to the Cultural Center bus stop and South Brisbane train station.


Do I need to RSVP?

Nope! This is a very casual meet-up. Most people just come to the ones that they are able to.


Do I have to stay for the whole 3 hour long meet-up?

Not at all. We book the area for the full time but most people end up coming and going as they please. It’s very chill.


What do I bring?

You can bring anything you have been working on, at any stage of completion or polish. Feel free to bring a laptop to show off work or get help from others. If you have a lot of gear, we suggest bringing a powerboard.


What if I don’t have anything to show?

Squiggly River is open to anyone, regardless of experience. You are absolutely welcome to just bring yourself, see what others have been working on and have a chat.


How can I find the group?

We will try to post the Window Bay number prior to the date. Otherwise we are usually the largest group of people at The Edge on Saturdays and we now have a nifty sign to look out for.


Is The Edge accessible?

Full accessibility details here.

  • The Edge is accessible via elevator.
  • Wheelchairs available from the SQL upon request.
  • Toilet facilities on every level.
  • Water bubblers of different heights.
  • Window bays have benches, tables, chairs and space for wheelchairs.


    • Food and drinks?

    The Edge has a small cafe inside it that has small snacks like brownies, muffins and biscuits (with Gluten-Free options).
